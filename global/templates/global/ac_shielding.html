AC ELF Shielding

AC ELF Magnetic Shielding is typically comprised of fully welded 1/4” aluminum plates. Aluminum is fastened to 3/4” plywood substrate. All seams, joints, corners, and butts are fully welded to ensure electrical conductivity. In some instances Low Carbon Steel is added to the design.

This Electromagnetic shielded enclosure prevent interference of the equipment within the room caused by power, data, telephone lines, or metallic paths. The shielded enclosure ensures the equipment will perform to its full potential.

Typical Applications

  • Electron microscope
  • Data centers
